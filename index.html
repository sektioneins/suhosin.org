About Suhosin-NG

Suhosin (pronounced 'su-ho-shin') is an advanced protection system for PHP installations. It is designed to protect servers and users from known and unknown flaws in PHP applications and the PHP core.

Suhosin-NG is heavily based on improving the Snuffleupagus PHP extension.

Development Status (2021)

Suhosin-NG is a work in progress. Even though there may be only few updates to our forked Snuffleupagus Github repository, there is a lot going on behind the curtains.

For more updates, please check out our News Section and our Twitter Feed.
